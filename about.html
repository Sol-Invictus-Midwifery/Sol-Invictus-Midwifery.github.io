My story

Ashley Baxter is a California licensed midwife who obtained a Bachelor's of Science in Midwifery from the MEAC accredited institution Midwive's College of Utah (MCU) in 2022. Her journey to Midwifery began in 2013 in Atlanta, Georgia where she learned the ancient art of being a labor support person. After spending four years serving in hospital settings, she came to the conclusion that birthing people and their families deserved an empowering and more informed experience. From here, she embarked on her path towards becoming a certified professional midwife with the objective of providing excellent midwifery care to birthing people.
