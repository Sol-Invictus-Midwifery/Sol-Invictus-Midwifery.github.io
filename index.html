My Mission

As a community midwife I strive to provide safe personalized midwifery care for low risk pregnancies, that meets your unique needs and fits your unique family. As your midwife and advocate for the community at large, I aim to educate and inform you of all your options in the childbearing year and beyond.

The midwifery model of care to which I subscribe, requires provider continuity of care, unequivocal informed choice, body autonomy and evidence based care.

At this time I provide midwifery care and well-person care in the greater San Diego area. I respecfully acknowledge that the area I serve is the occupied unceded land of the Kumeyaay/Kumiais people, who have long been the caretakers of this land. To learn more about this first nation click here.
